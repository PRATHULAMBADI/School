APPLY FOR FREE AND REDUCED MEALS TODAY!

Applying for the Free and Reduced Meal Program is easyand fast.Families wishing to receive these benifits in the 2020 school year will have to fillout the online application*. Srart the process by following these simple steps:

  1. Go to pfisd.net and click onDepartment(A) and then on Food Services(b).
  2. select Free and Reduced Meals Application(c) from the column on the left.
  3. Chooseone of the three application languages(D) available.(A new window will open,taking you the begining of the application.)
  4. Follow the instructions there, starting by clicking on the BEGIN APPLICATION PROCESS(E) button.
A FEW THINGS TO CONSIDER:
  1. The help column(F)on the right is available to you at all times, and provides information corresponding to the screen you are in.
  2. When promoted,provide your email address.This facilitates communication regarding your application's status.
  3. Take note of the confirmation numbergiven at the end of the appication. This is the only reference you'll have when tracking the status of your application.
  4. If you need further assistance,contact:
