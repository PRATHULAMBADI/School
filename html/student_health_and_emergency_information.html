Authorization for Treatment at School

Treatments to be performed during the school day require a physician's order and written parent consent. School health protocol signed by a Physician, allows for first aid treatment by the campus nurse. This treatment may include topical over-the-counter medications.

In addition, oral medications may be given per standing delegated orders, with parent permission, in limited situations. Parents will be called for permission prior to administrator of any oral medication to your child.
